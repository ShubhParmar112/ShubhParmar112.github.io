Who I Am

About Me

I'm a B.Tech Data Science student at MPSTME, NMIMS University, Mumbai. My journey in tech began with a curiosity about how data can drive decisions and has since evolved into a strong interest in building intelligent, research-driven systems.

My work focuses on machine learning & statistical analysis, particularly in audio-based classification and real-world data modeling. I've worked on projects in emotion recognition and signal-based classification, applying techniques like neural networks, feature extraction, and statistical testing (ANOVA, MANOVA, t-tests) to uncover meaningful insights.

Beyond building models, I enjoy exploring the reasoning behind them — whether through research, experimentation, or analyzing results in depth. I believe in continuous learning and giving my 100% to everything I do, approaching each project with discipline, curiosity, and intent.
